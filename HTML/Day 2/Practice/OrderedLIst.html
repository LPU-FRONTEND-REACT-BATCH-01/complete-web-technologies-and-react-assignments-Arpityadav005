Ordered List

  1. Wash and soak rice for 20–30 minutes.
  2. Marinate chicken or mutton with curd and spices.
  3. Heat oil and fry onions until golden brown.
  4. Add whole spices like bay leaf, cloves, and cardamom.
  5. Add marinated meat and cook until half done.
  6. Boil rice separately until 70% cooked.
  7. Layer rice over the cooked meat.
  8. Add saffron milk, mint, and coriander leaves.
  9. Cover and cook on low heat (dum).
  10. Mix gently and serve hot.
